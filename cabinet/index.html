THE WHOLE TRICK

Unrecorded is unknowable. The orders happened either way — you watched them arrive. But if nothing wrote them down, there is nothing to look up, and no amount of asking nicely brings them back.

It answers anyway. That's the dangerous part. You don't get "I don't know" — you get a confident number in the right shape, and it changes every time you ask.

With the table, it stops guessing and starts counting. It finds the customer's rows, adds them, and shows you which rows it used. You can check its work, because the rows are right there.

The table is yours, not the AI's. It outlives the chat, the memory, and the machine. That's what makes it the source of truth — it stays right whether or not anything is running.
